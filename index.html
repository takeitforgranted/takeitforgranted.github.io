About Us

Who We Are

Podillia Avia started as a small group of flight sim pilots who wanted something more structured than flying alone. Today it's a growing crew flying scheduled routes, tracking real career progress, and sharing the hobby with people who take it just seriously enough.

Whether you're new to virtual aviation or a veteran of the skies, there's a seat in the cockpit for you.
